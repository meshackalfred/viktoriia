About me

I'm a pianist with 21 years of experience, who began musical education at the age of 4. I was born on September 24, 1993. At the age of 6, I played my first concert with a chamber orchestra directed by Galina Osadcze. I am a laureate of many Ukrainian and Polish piano and chamber competitions, including the winner of the grand prix "Golden lira" in Ukraine, 1st place in International competition of musical interpretation im. Wanda Wiłkomirska, 3rd place in Kameralny intercubular competition in Łódź (All in all 27 awards and distinctions as of today). I promote Polish music, especially Fryderyk Chopin, Grażyna Bacewicz and Karol Szymanowski. As part of cooperation with the Krakow chamber band, I perform periodic concerts in the center of Podgórze culture. I started my studies at the Academy of Music in Krakow, in the piano class of prof. Ewa Bukajemska in 2012. Music is my true passion. I was a participant in many concerts with the presence of outstanding Ukrainian musicians, including Harys Szyriński and Anatoli Awdijewski. I participated in many concerts at the municipal opera in Odessa. I regularly perform at festivals and concerts in European countries. I also took a part in the organization of performances during the District Society of Poles in Ukraine, while participating in recordings in the local radio Promiń, Skifija, WTW. I constantly improve my skills by expanding the repertoire in both solo and chamber music. I try to combine different styles of the plays with particular emphasis on chamber music and solo music of the eighteenth-twentieth century, because it occupies a very important place within my pianistic interests. Viktoriia Karpenko is constantly developing her skills as a soloist and chamber musician.
